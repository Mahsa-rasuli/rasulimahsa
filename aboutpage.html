Ball Games

Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of nine players each. The goal is to score runs by hitting a thrown ball and touching a series of four bases arranged at the corners of a 90-foot diamond.

Soccer(football)

Soccer, known as football in most countries outside of North America, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. Each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court under organized rules.
